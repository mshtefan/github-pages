02. Interact

Our platform provides an easy-to-use and flexible UI for the management of the motivation program which allows to easily control the goals, set up mechanics and collect analytics.

  • Employee database
  • Goal management
  • Communication and alerts
  • KPI constructor
The platform provides access to all information about individual employees, their progress towards goals, metrics and overall performance statistics.
Flexible goal management system allows to create recurring performance goals for employees, departments and positions with on the go customization abilities.
Communication automation ensures that engaged staff and management recieve timely notifications about their performance and progress providing additional tranparency to daily operations.
Complex formulas can be set up using a flexible KPI constructor to collect real time aggregated metrics based on any of the available employee data.
03. Recognize

We provide a white-labelled employee-facing profile page, with effortless and involving UX, delivering a transparent and hands-on motivation flow.

Profile

A white-labeled, personalised profile page with employee's progress towards KPIs, reached and available milestones as well as custom communication actions.

Goals

Interactive goals tailored for the program structure, including social media activities, performance requirements, feedback surveys, public appearances and other milestones that deserve recognition.

Benefits

The heart of the employee motivation program, ranging from gifts and coupon codes to services and company-level recognitions.

Gamification

Numerous gamification mechanics, including badges, tiers, leaderboards and company-wide achievements. Attract wider employee base by diversifying the goals in an appealing way.

04. Evaluate
Segmentation

Set up in-depth segmentation rules based on employees actions, their frequency, sequence, quantity or relation to each other. Use advanced filtering mechanism to create rules tailored specifically for the intents relevant to your company.

Manager alerts

Be informed about your staff performance proactively. Receive notifications when your employees over-perform, reach new performance milestones or mov into certain segment groups.

Reports

Build industry recognized custom reports based on the data aggregated in the platform. Set up automatic generation and delivery, or reach the analytics on demand.
